Dark Souls' History

Hidetaka Miyazaki

The first Dark Souls was released in 2011, Dark Souls 2 was released during 2014, Dark Souls 3 was released during 2016, and Dark souls Remastered was released during 2018. The creator of the Dark Souls Franchise is Hidetaka Miyazaki. It was developed by the game company called “From Software”. Other games that they created related to the franchise is Bloodborne and Demon’s Souls. The Dark Souls Franchise sold a total of 25 million games.
