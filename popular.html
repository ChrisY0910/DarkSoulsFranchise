Dark Souls' Popularity

Dark Souls is one of the most popular RPG in the world. RPG stands for Role Playing Game. Dark Souls is very popular because the graphics of the game are one of the most detailed and most beautiful in gaming history. Dark Souls is also one of the hardest games in gaming history. Ranking in the top 25 hardest games. In addition, Dark Souls is popular because many famous gamers like Pewdiepie, Jacksepticeye, Markiplier, and more. The game is also hard to play meaning that with practice you can beat the bosses in the game and in the end feel very satisfied. The game is challenging so worrying about finishing the game quickly is never a thought. Its an extremely fun game.
